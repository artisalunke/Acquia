`custom report </lift/profile-mgr/analytics/dashboards/custom>`__, you
can `download the report's data <#downloading-custom-report-data>`__, make additional `changes
to your reports <#editing-custom-reports>`__, or `delete reports <#deleting-custom-reports>`__ as needed.

Downloading custom report data
------------------------------

You can export or download your data in several file formats to send to
colleagues or to import into other systems. After creating a custom
report (as described in `Creating custom reports <#create>`__), perform
the following actions to download the report's data:

#. Click the **gear** icon located to the right of the **Run** button,
   and then click **Download**.

   |Downloading custom data|

#. In the **File Format** list, click your desired file format.

   |Downloading window|

#. Customize the **Results**, **Values**, **Limit**, and **Filename**
   based on your requirements.
#. To save the results of your custom filter, select from one of the
   following steps based on your needs:

   -  **Open in Browser** - View the results in a new browser window
   -  **Download** - Save the results as file on your computer

Editing custom reports
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To edit an existing custom report, perform the following steps:

#. Sign in to your |acquia-product:lpm| user interface.
#. In the top menu, click **Analytics**.
#. Select the category in which your report appears.
#. Click the gear button next to the report you want to modify, and then
   click **Edit**.
#. To edit the title of your report, click the **Pencil** icon next to
   the title, make your changes, and click **Update**.
#. After making your changes to your report, click **Save Report**.

Deleting custom reports
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To delete an existing custom report, perform the following steps:

#. Sign in to your |acquia-product:lpm| user interface.
#. In the top menu, click **Analytics**.
#. Select the category in which your report appears.
#. Click the custom report you want to delete, and then click the
   **Delete** link.
#. Click **OK**.
