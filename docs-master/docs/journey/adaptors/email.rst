Email-related adaptors
======================

.. toctree::
   :hidden:
   :glob:

   /journey/adaptors/email/*

|acquia-product:aj| enables you to send personalized emails to your
customers, based upon each customer's unique journey. Your
`graphs </journey/getting-started/graph>`__ in your customer journey can
send emails using the `write adaptor </journey/adaptors/email/write>`__.
