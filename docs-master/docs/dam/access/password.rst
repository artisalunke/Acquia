Changing or resetting your password
===================================

Regardless of the reason, both |acquia-product:dam| and
|acquia-product:bc| allow you to modify your password or obtain a new
one, if required.

.. _changedam:

Changing your |acquia-product:dam| password
-------------------------------------------

To change your |acquia-product:dam| password, complete the following
steps:

#. Sign in to |acquia-product:dam|.
#. Click your name in the top-right corner, and then click **Profile**.
#. Click the **Change password** link under your email address.
#. Enter your new password, and then click **Save**.

.. _changeconnect:

Changing your |acquia-product:bc| password
------------------------------------------

To change your |acquia-product:bc| password, complete the following
steps:

#. Sign in to |acquia-product:bc|.
#. Click your icon/avatar in the top-right corner and select
   **Profile**.
#. Click the **Change password** link under your email address.
#. Enter your new password and click **Save**.

.. _reset:

Resetting your password
-----------------------

If you have forgotten your password, complete the following steps to
reset the password for your account:

#. On the |acquia-product:dam| sign in page, click the **Forgot your
   password?** link.
#. Enter your email address, and then click **Submit**.

|acquia-product:dam| will send you an email that includes password-reset
instructions.
