Managing contacts with the address book
=======================================

You can save the email addresses of your contacts to your
|acquia-product:dam| and |acquia-product:bc| address book to quickly
share assets and initiate approval requests.

.. _add:

Adding contacts
---------------

Sending or sharing an asset or lightbox with a user will automatically
add that user to your address book.

#. Use the following procedure to access the Address Book, based on how
   you're using the product:

   -  *|acquia-product:dam|* - Sign in to |acquia-product:dam|, click
      your name in the top-right corner, and then click **Address
      Book**.
   -  *|acquia-product:bc|* - Sign in to |acquia-product:bc|, click your
      account avatar in the top-right corner, and then click
      **Profile**. In the Profile window, click **Address Book**.

#. Enter the contact’s email address, and click **Enter**.
#. Click **OK** to save.

|Address book|

.. _remove:

Removing contacts
-----------------

#. Use the following procedure to access the address book, based on how
   you're using the product:

   -  *|acquia-product:dam|* - Sign in to |acquia-product:dam|, click
      your name in the top-right corner, and then click **Address
      Book**.
   -  *|acquia-product:bc|* - Sign in to |acquia-product:bc|, click your
      account avatar in the top-right corner, and then click
      **Profile**. In the Profile window, click **Address Book**.

#. Click the minus sign ( **-** ) to the right of the contact’s email
   address.
#. Click **OK** to confirm the changes.

.. _all:

Selecting contacts from the address book
----------------------------------------

When `emailing a link or download </dam/content/asset/share>`__ in
|acquia-product:dam| or initiating `an approval
request </dam/admin/approve>`__, click the **To** field in the email
window. This will display a dropdown of the users in your address book.
Either select an email address or enter an address to fine-tune your
selections.
