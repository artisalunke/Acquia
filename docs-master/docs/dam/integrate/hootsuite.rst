Integration: Hootsuite
======================

Click, post, done! The |acquia-product:dam| plug-in for Hootsuite
creates a seamless connection between |acquia-product:dam| and
Hootsuite. In seconds, you can pull engaging images from your DAM
directly into your posts and share it with followers across your social
channels.

To install and use the |acquia-product:dam| plug-in with Hootsuite,
complete the following steps:

#. Sign in to Hootsuite.
#. Click the app directory icon |App Directory|.
#. Search for |acquia-product:dam|, and then click **Install App**.
#. After clicking **Finish**, you will be prompted to enter your
   company's |acquia-product:dam| domain.

   .. note::
      Some organizations may require additional authentication, such as
      your individual sign-in credentials. Follow the displayed prompts to
      connect |acquia-product:dam|.

#. After installation, click the publisher icon |Publisher|. Under
   **Content Sources**, click |acquia-product:dam| to access your
   |acquia-product:dam| library.

   |Hootsuite Folders|

#. Select one or more images that you want to include in your post. If
   selecting multiple images, they must be in the same folder â€”
   selections reset when you change folders.

   .. note::
      Only image files are available using the |acquia-product:dam| plug-in
      for Hootsuite.

#. After selection, the images you have chosen will appear in the
   **Compose** window. Compose your message as normal, and then post to
   your selected channels.
