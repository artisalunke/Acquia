Tutorial Goal
-------------

*Create a personalized call-to-action based on your audience’s location
and more with |acquia-product:cha|.*

Tutorial Overview
-----------------

This tutorial is a step-by-step, hands-on guide on how to create a
personalization with |acquia-product:cha|. In this example Lift will
identify your audience based on their location, returning visitation
status, and serve a relevant banner and call-to-action in real-time.

.. raw:: html

   <iframe allowfullscreen="" class="shadow bord" frameborder="0" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/t-xWTQOwMzI?rel=0" width="800" height="480"></iframe>

|acquia-product:cha| unifies content and data to serve targeted,
personalized content to visitors. With |acquia-product:cha|, content
managers can track visitor behavior throughout your site, and deliver
in-context, personalized experiences.
