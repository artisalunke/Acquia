Archiving data in Acquia DAM¶

This is an add-on feature. Contact your Account Manager to add this feature to your subscription.

The information on this page applies only to users that have `*Admin </dam/admin/users#role>`__ or *Contributor* role type permissions.*

Archival storage allows you to store and manage your rarely-used files, while keeping them organized, close-at-hand, and easy to find and access.

Assets that are archived are inactive — therefore not viewable in|acquia-product:bc| — and will display an archive icon on the asset thumbnail. After an asset is archived, users cannot download the asset until it is unarchived (which typically takes approximately 24 hours).

Notes

  • Metadata cannot be added to archived assets.
  • By default, only assets that are not archived will show up in search results. However, you can filter for archived assets.

Archiving assets¶

To archive an asset, complete the following steps:

  1. Sign in to Acquia DAM.
  2. Select one or more assets that you want to archive.
  3. Click the archive icon in the actions toolbar, and then click Archive.

Assets are typically archived within 24 hours.

Unarchiving assets¶

To unarchive an asset, and make it once again available for use, complete the following steps:

  1. Sign in to Acquia DAM.
  2. Select one ore more archived assets that you want to unarchive.
  3. Click the archive icon in the actions toolbar, and then click Unarchive.

During the unarchiving process, assets will display a grayed archive icon on the asset thumbnail. Assets are typically unarchived within 24 hours.
