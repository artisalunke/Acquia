Custom pages in Brand Connect¶

With Acquia DAM, you can create custom pages. Custom pages are often used to convey non-brand guidelines information to your users. Some examples include a contact page, answers to frequently asked questions, or a getting-started guide.

  • Creating a page
  • Editing a page
  • Deleting a page
  • Navigation

Creating a page¶

To create a custom page for Acquia DAM:

  1. Sign in to Acquia DAM.
  2. Click Brands in the top navigation and then select Edit for the Brand Connect portal you want to configure.
  3. Click Pages in the top navigation.
  4. Click ADD.
  5. Enter a title for the custom page.
  6. Click ADD.

Editing a page¶

You can edit a page that has been created.

  1. Sign in to Acquia DAM.
  2. Click Brands in the top navigation and then select Edit for the Brand Connect portal you want to configure.
  3. Click Pages in the top navigation.
  4. Hover over the page title and then click the pencil icon .
  5. Enter the information into the editor box, or click ``</> CODE`` to insert HTML or CSS.
  6. Click Preview to view your changes, or Publish to publish them.
  7. When you are finished, click Exit.

The page should now be created, and a link is automatically added to the navigation.

Deleting a page¶

If a page is no longer needed, it can be deleted.

  1. Sign in to Acquia DAM.
  2. Click Brands in the top navigation and then select Edit for the Brand Connect portal you want to configure.
  3. Click Pages on the top navigation.
  4. Hover your mouse over the page title and click the Trash icon .
  5. Click the Delete button to confirm.

Navigation¶

Sign in to Acquia DAM.

Click Brands in the top navigation and then select Edit for the Brand Connect portal you want to configure.

Click Navigation in the top navigation.

Update the navigation:

  • Rename: You can rename any page or link by highlighting the title and entering the new name that you want to display.
  • Re-order: : You can change the order of pages and links in the navigation by hovering over the title then dragging the move icon to reorder.
  • Remove: You can remove a page or link from the navigation by hovering your mouse over the title and then clicking the trash icon . A custom page will not be deleted, it will just be removed from the navigation.
  • Add a page: You can add previously removed pages back to your navigation by clicking Add a page and selecting the page. Newly created custom pages will automatically be added to your navigation.
  • Add a link: You can add a link to any URL on your navigation by clicking Add a link. Enter the link name and url, then click Add.
  • Edit a link or page: You can edit a page or link by hovering your mouse over the title and clicking the Pencil icon . Make your edits and click Save.
