Completing the process¶

Custom domains

  • Prepare
  • Point
  • Verify
  • Email
  • Complete

Complete the following steps to add a custom domain name to your website, depending on the features that you have installed:

  • Professional fonts
  • External Links module

Professional fonts¶

If you are using professional fonts on your website, you must reconfigure your external font provider account (including Typekit and Fonts.com) to include your new domain name.

Typekit domain name change¶

Typekit maps your account to one or more domain names. After you point a custom domain name to your Acquia Cloud Site Factory website, you must reconfigure your Typekit kit.

  1. Click Launch Kit Editor to change the settings for your account.

  2. Add the new domain name in the Domains text field, deleting the domain name you will no longer be using if necessary.

External Links module¶

If your website uses the External Links module, validate your website’s external links pattern matching to ensure that it’s configured to handle new custom domains.

For more information, see External links.

You’re done!¶

Your Acquia Cloud Site Factory website or site collection is now available to your site visitors at your custom domain!
