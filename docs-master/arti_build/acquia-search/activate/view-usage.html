Viewing search usage and statistics¶

Through the use of the Acquia Cloud interface, you can learn more about your Acquia Search subscription and its usage.

Viewing your Acquia Search usage¶

To view information about your use of Acquia Search, complete the following steps:

  1. Sign in to your Acquia subscription.

  2. Select your application, and then click its Acquia Search link.

  3. In the upper right corner, click Search Stats.

The table that appears on the Search page displays how much of your Acquia Search subscription you are using, including the following attributes:

  • Document Count - The number of documents that are in your search index
  • Query Count - The number of executed searches
  • Index Size (MB) - The amount of storage that your search index uses, in megabytes

Note

The Search Core Name column displays all provisioned indexes, whether or not they are in use.

The information displayed in the table includes how your website’s searches, documents, and storage quantities have changed from month to month, which can assist you in determining how changes in your website may have affected changes in your Acquia Search resources. If you have more than one search index, the table combines the statistics for all of your provisioned search indexes — this can help you plan whether you might need to upgrade or downgrade your Acquia Search subscription.
