|acquia-product:ac| onboarding guide
====================================

.. toctree::

Welcome to Acquia’s platform and tools. Whether you are deploying a new
application or migrating an existing one, if you enlist the services of
`|acquia-product:onb| <https://www.acquia.com/customer-success/acquia-ready-services>`__,
our team of experts will guide you through all the important activities
along your critical path to launch.

In addition to the extensive documentation available on the `Acquia
|acquia-product:lib| </acquia-cloud>`__, this guide should serve as your
starting point to ensure your application is tuned and optimized for
success on |acquia-product:ac|.

-  `What to expect during onboarding </acquia-cloud/ready/expect>`__
-  `Getting your team ready </acquia-cloud/ready/getting-team-ready>`__
-  `Three pillars of a successful
   launch </acquia-cloud/ready/three-pillars>`__
-  `Developing on
   |acquia-product:ac| </acquia-cloud/ready/developing>`__

.. _engagements:

Additional engagements
----------------------

In addition to normal launch support, |acquia-product:onb| also offers
the following engagements:

-  After Hours Launch Support - For website launches that occur on
   weekends or outside of a customer's normal business hours,
   |acquia-product:onb| can provide on-call (remote) support for up to
   four hours.
-  `Custom VCL </acquia-cloud/ready/customvcl>`__ (Varnish Configuration
   Language)
-  `|acquia-product:onb| SimpleSAMLphp
   engagement </acquia-cloud/ready/saml>`__
-  `Migration Services </guide/migrate>`__

For additional assistance, contact your account manager.
