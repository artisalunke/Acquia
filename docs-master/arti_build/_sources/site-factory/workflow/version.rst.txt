Determining your platform version
=================================

As a part of any `scheduled platform maintenance or
hotfixes </site-factory/workflow/deployments>`__ for
|acquia-product:edg|, you can verify your platform version number to
confirm if maintenance has been completed on your subscription.

.. _access:

Viewing your platform version
-----------------------------

To view current information regarding your |acquia-product:edg| platform
version, complete the following steps:

#. `Sign in to the Factory interface </site-factory/manage/login>`__
   with either the *`release
   engineer </site-factory/users/admin/release-engineer>`__* or
   *`developer </site-factory/users/admin/developer>`__* role.
#. In the admin menu, click **Administration**.
#. Click the **Factory information** link.

Your platform version is displayed as **Installed |acquia-product:edg|
version**.

A list of the user-facing changes in each release is available in the
`|acquia-product:edg| release notes </site-factory/release-notes>`__.
