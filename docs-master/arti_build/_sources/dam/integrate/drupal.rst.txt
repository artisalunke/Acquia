|acquia-product:dam| Drupal integration
=======================================

.. toctree::
   :hidden:
   :glob:

   /dam/integrate/drupal/*

With the |acquia-product:dam| Connector for Drupal 8, you can sync your
digital assets with your Drupal website, and seamlessly use content from
your |acquia-product:dam| instance within all the websites you maintain.

For Drupal 8.4 websites, download the |connector|_ or view
the `documentation </dam/integrate/drupal/d8>`__.

.. |connector| replace::
  \ |acquia-product:dam|\  Connector for Drupal 8.4
.. _connector:
  https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/modules/media-acquia-dam
