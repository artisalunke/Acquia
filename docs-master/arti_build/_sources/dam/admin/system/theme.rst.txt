Changing your |acquia-product:dam| theme
========================================

.. container:: message-status

  This feature is available only to |acquia-product:dam| Professional
  subscribers.

You can use Themes to brand the |acquia-product:dam| experience by
customizing the header and email logos and colors.

.. _configure:

Configure the header
--------------------

You can customize the logo and colors of the top navigation in
|acquia-product:dam|.

#. Sign in to |acquia-product:dam|.
#. Click on the top navigation and select **System Preferences**.

   |Themes preferences|

#. Click **Themes** in the left navigation panel.
#. Click the dropdown next to **Editing** and select **Header**, if it’s
   not already selected.
#. Hover your mouse over the |acquia-product:dam| logo (or your
   previously configured logo) and click **Change Logo**.
#. This opens the **Asset Chooser**. Navigate through the folders or
   search for the image you want to use use for your header.
#. Once the logo is selected, click **Insert**.

.. note::
  Use a PNG, JPG, or JPEG for the logo.

Colors
------

#. Sign in to |acquia-product:dam|.
#. Click on the top navigation and select **System Preferences**.

   |Themes preferences|

#. Click **Themes** in the left navigation panel.
#. Click the drop-down menu next to **Background color** and/or **Link
   color**.
#. Select a spot on the color chooser or enter the hex code for your
   preferred color. Note: Make sure your link color contrasts the header
   color, so viewers will be able to see the links.
#. Click **Save**.
#. Not happy with the changes? Click **Reset** to restore the original
   colors and |acquia-product:dam| logo.
#. After you are satisfied with the changes, click **Save**.

.. _email:

Configure email
---------------

You can customize the logo, signature and colors of system-generated
emails.

#. Sign into |acquia-product:dam|.
#. Click the |Settings| **Settings** icon on the top navigation and
   select **System Preferences**.
#. Click **Themes** in the left navigation panel.
#. Click the dropdown next to **Editing** and select **Email**.

Logo
----

#. Sign in to |acquia-product:dam|.
#. Click on the top navigation and select **System Preferences**.

   |Themes preferences|

#. Click **Themes** in the left navigation panel.
#. Click the dropdown next to **Editing** and select **Header**, if it’s
   not already selected.
#. Hover your mouse over the middle header and click **Change Logo**.
#. This opens the **Asset Chooser**. Navigate through the folders or
   search for the image you want to use use for your email header.
#. Once the image is selected, click **Insert**.

.. note::
  Use a PNG, JPG, or JPEG for the logo.

Signature
---------

#. Sign in to |acquia-product:dam|.
#. Click on the top navigation and select **System Preferences**.

   |Themes preferences|

#. Click **Themes** in the left navigation panel.
#. Click the dropdown next to **Editing** and select **Email**.
#. Hover your mouse over the |acquia-product:dam| signature.
#. Click anywhere in the black box to edit the signature text.
#. Enter the new signature.
#. Header color:

   #. Click the drop-down menu next to **Header color** on the bottom of
      the page.
   #. Select a spot on the color chooser or enter the hex code for your
      brand color.
   #. Click **Save**.

#. Click **Send Test** to have an example email sent to the `admin email
   notification address </dam/admin/system>`__ to verify it looks
   accurate.
#. Not happy with the changes? Click **Reset** to restore the original
   colors, signature and logo.
#. After you are satisfied with the changes, click **Save**.
