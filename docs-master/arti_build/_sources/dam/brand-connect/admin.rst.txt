Administering |acquia-product:bc|
=================================

|acquia-product:dam| |acquia-product:bc| is smart, simple
brand-management technology built for the lightning-fast pace of digital
marketing. You can use it to post easily adaptable and distributable
brand guidelines and automate brand-management tasks, accelerating
marketing operations and creative cycles.

As the |acquia-product:bc| admin, you can complete several
administrative tasks, including adding and editing brand guidelines,
altering the appearance of your brand portal, and configuring the
|acquia-product:bc| settings:

-  `Sign in <#sign-in>`__
-  `Brand guidelines <#guidelines>`__
-  `Custom pages <#pages>`__
-  `Brand portal navigation <#nav>`__
-  `Brand portal appearance <#appearance>`__
-  `HTML tags to avoid <#html>`__
-  `Brand portal settings <#settings>`__

.. _sign-in:

Signing in to |acquia-product:bc|
---------------------------------

To sign in to |acquia-product:bc|, complete the following steps:

#. `Sign in to |acquia-product:dam| </dam/access>`__.
#. Click in the top menu, click **Brands**.
#. Click the brand you want to manage.

.. _guidelines:

Adding and editing brand guidelines
-----------------------------------

#. Click **Guidelines** in the top navigation.
#. Click **Add** underneath the **Brand Guidelines** headline.
#. Enter a title for the guidelines, then click the **Add** button.
#. To add content to your brand guidelines, hover your mouse over the
   title and click the **Pencil** icon |pencil icon|.
#. To delete the brand guidelines, click the **Trash** icon |trash icon|
   when you hover over the page title.

.. _pages:

Adding and editing custom pages
-------------------------------

#. Click **Pages** in the top navigation.
#. Click **Add** underneath the **Pages** headline.
#. Enter a title for the page, and then click the **Add** button.
#. Add content to your custom pages by hovering your mouse over the
   title and clicking the **Pencil** icon |pencil icon|.
#. To delete the custom page, click the **Trash** icon |trash icon| when
   you hover over the page title.

For more information, see `configuring custom
pages </dam/brand-connect/custom>`__.

.. _nav:

Edit the brand portal's navigation
----------------------------------

#. Click **Navigation** in the top navigation.
#. Click **Add a page** to insert a link to one of your custom pages.
   The custom page must be created before adding it to the navigation.
#. Click **Add a link** to insert a link to any URL.
#. Change the order of pages and links in the navigation by hovering
   over the title and then dragging the **Move** icon |Move icon| to
   reorder.

.. _appearance:

Manage the brand portal's appearance
------------------------------------

Click **Appearance** in the top navigation, and then select the part of
the portal that you want to change:

-  **Font** - Styles all text throughout your portal in the selected
   font. Supports 800+ Google fonts.
-  **Header** - Edit the background color, link color, and height of
   your brand portal header.
-  **Footer** - Change the copy at the bottom of your brand portal.
-  **Homepage** - Add design, copy, and functionality elements using
   code and widgets.
-  **Login** - Insert a logo, header, background image, and color to
   your brand portalâ€™s sign-in page.

.. _html:

HTML tags to avoid
------------------

Avoid using the following custom HTML tags, as they can cause errors
when displaying or editing |acquia-product:bc|:

-  ``<html>``
-  ``<head>``
-  ``<body>``
-  ``<!DOCTYPE>``
-  ``<style>`` (Instead, enter CSS code directly in the CSS tab.)
-  Do not reference external JavaScript libraries.

.. _settings:

Editing the brand portal's settings
-----------------------------------

#. In the top menu, click **Settings**.
#. Change the title of the portal, adjust security permissions, set the
   language, and other settings.
#. To save your changes, click **Save**.
