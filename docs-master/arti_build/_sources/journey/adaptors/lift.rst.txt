|acquia-product:cha|-related adaptors
=====================================

.. toctree::
   :hidden:
   :glob:

   /journey/adaptors/lift/*

|lift|_ allows you to tailor experiences on
your web properties. When combined with |acquia-product:aj|, you can
provide web content to visitors that is relevant and customized, based
on other actions the visitor has taken outside of your website, as part
of their customer journey with your brand.

.. |lift| replace:: \ |acquia-product:cha|\ 
.. _lift: /lift

The following adaptors are available interact with |acquia-product:cha|
from your `graphs </journey/getting-started/graph>`__:

-  `Capture </journey/adaptors/lift/capture>`__ - Store journey
   information in |acquia-product:cha| as your customer journey executes
-  `Content </journey/adaptors/lift/content>`__ - Retrieve a content
   item from |lift ch|_ using information
   gathered as your customer journey executes
-  `Decide </journey/adaptors/lift/decide>`__ - Provide content for
   a unique user and a particular content
   `slot </lift/exp-builder/slots/>`__, based on the
   `segments </lift/profile-mgr/segment/>`__ to which the vistor's
   profile belongs
-  `Visitor </journey/adaptors/lift/visitor>`__ - Retrieve a
   specific value from |acquia-product:cha| related to a visitor's
   profile, such as the account, email, or a custom data value

.. |lift ch| replace:: \ |acquia-product:ch|\ 
.. _lift ch: /lift/contenthub
