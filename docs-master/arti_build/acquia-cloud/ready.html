Acquia Cloud onboarding guide¶

Welcome to Acquia’s platform and tools. Whether you are deploying a new application or migrating an existing one, if you enlist the services of |acquia-product:onb|, our team of experts will guide you through all the important activities along your critical path to launch.

In addition to the extensive documentation available on the Acquia |acquia-product:lib|, this guide should serve as your starting point to ensure your application is tuned and optimized for success on Acquia Cloud.

Additional engagements¶

In addition to normal launch support, Acquia Ready also offers the following engagements:

  • After Hours Launch Support - For website launches that occur on weekends or outside of a customer’s normal business hours, Acquia Ready can provide on-call (remote) support for up to four hours.
  • Custom VCL (Varnish Configuration Language)
  • |acquia-product:onb| SimpleSAMLphp engagement
  • Migration Services

For additional assistance, contact your account manager.
